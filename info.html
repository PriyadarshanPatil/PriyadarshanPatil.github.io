General

I am currently in the fourth year of the Doctoral program at The University of Texas at Austin. I am majoring in Operations Research and Industrial Engineering (ORIE) and my specialization is network optimization. My academic interests include Transportation network analysis & optimization, network flow theory, algorithms and their implementations, and data science. UT Austin has also given me the opportunity to explore engineering education research as well as teaching and assessment techniques as an instructor. You can download my CV here.

I play racquetball competitively, having won the UT Mens Intramural tournament for Racquetball singles, as well as winning my brackets at multiple tournaments around Texas. The next goal is to represent UT Austin nationally at the Intercollegiate Nationals 2022, to be held at Louisiana State University, Baton Rouge in March 2022. The 2020 championship was cancelled due to COVID-19.
